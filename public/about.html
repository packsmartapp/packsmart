About Us

Plan, book & pack — all in one place

PackSmart is your all-in-one travel companion. Get a personalized itinerary, packing list, visa briefing, and local tips in seconds — then search flights, compare hotels, and book tours without leaving the site.

Why we built this

We've all been there — frantically Googling "do I need a visa for Thailand" at midnight, forgetting chargers, or showing up to a monsoon with flip-flops. Travel preparation shouldn't be stressful, scattered across 20 browser tabs, or something you skip entirely.

PackSmart was built to solve that. Tell us where you're going, when, how you're getting there, and what you plan to do. PackSmart analyzes your trip details and generates a complete preparation plan — personalized to your specific journey. No sign-ups, no downloads, no fluff.

But preparation is only half the journey. That's why PackSmart also lets you search flights across 100+ airlines, compare hotel prices, discover tours and activities, and access destination guides — so you can go from planning to booking in one seamless flow.
