Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: March 22, 2026

In short: Some links on PackSmart earn us a commission at no extra cost to you. This helps us keep the site free and the guides coming.

How PackSmart Earns Revenue

PackSmart is free to use. We earn revenue through affiliate partnerships with travel brands. When you click certain links on our site and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission from the partner company. This never costs you anything extra — the price you pay is the same whether you use our link or go directly.

Our Affiliate Partners

We currently partner with:

  • SafetyWing (travel insurance)
  • Yesim (eSIM data plans)
  • GetYourGuide (tours and activities)
  • Welcome Pickups (airport transfers)
  • Localrent (car rentals)
  • Klook (tours and activities in Asia)
  • Kiwi.com (flights)
  • Tiqets (attraction tickets)
  • Plus flight and hotel metasearch powered by Travelpayouts

How We Choose What to Recommend

We recommend products and services that we believe provide genuine value to travelers. Affiliate partnerships do not influence our editorial content. We will never recommend a product solely because it pays a higher commission. If a product isn't worth recommending, we won't feature it regardless of the affiliate relationship.

What This Means for You

When you see links to travel services in our blog posts and guides, some of those are affiliate links. You can identify them by the small disclosure note at the top of each article and in our affiliate grids. Using these links is entirely optional and costs you nothing extra. If you find our guides helpful, using our affiliate links is a great way to support PackSmart at no cost to you.
