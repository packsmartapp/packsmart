Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼
Yes, you can cancel your Pro or Travel Agent subscription at any time. No long-term contracts, no cancellation fees. Your access continues until the end of your billing period. If you've purchased the Lifetime plan, it's yours forever.
Do you offer annual discounts? ▼
Yes! Our annual plans come with significant savings. Pro annual saves you 58% compared to monthly billing ($24.99/year vs $4.99/month), and Travel Agent annual saves 37% ($179.99/year vs $24.99/month). We also offer a Lifetime option for those who want permanent access.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and UPI payments for Indian customers. All transactions are secure and encrypted with industry-standard SSL protocols.
Can I upgrade from Free to Pro anytime? ▼
Absolutely! You can start with the free plan and upgrade to Pro whenever you're ready. All your existing plans, saved destinations, and history will be preserved. You'll immediately get access to all Pro features.
Is there a free trial for Pro features? ▼
Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial of Pro features so you can explore everything before committing to a paid plan. No credit card required to start the trial. If you don't upgrade, you'll automatically go back to the Free plan.
What's included in the Travel Agent plan? ▼
The Travel Agent plan is designed for travel professionals and agencies. It includes everything in Pro plus white-label capabilities (add your logo and branding), client-facing PDF exports, unlimited plan generation, a simple CRM to save client details, bulk generation tools, custom domain support, and priority email and chat support.
