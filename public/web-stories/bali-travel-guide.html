Bali Travel Guide 2026

7 tips every Indian traveler needs to know
VISA

Visa-on-Arrival (VOA)

Get your visa at the airport for IDR 500,000 (₹2,700). Single entry, 30 days extendable to 60.
BEST TIME

April to October

Dry season brings perfect weather. May–June & September are ideal for fewer crowds and great prices.
BUDGET

₹50,000–80,000 for 7 Days

Meals run ₹150–500 per plate. Bali offers incredible value with beachfront accommodation from ₹1,500–3,000/night.
PACKING TIP

Sarongs Are Mandatory

Required for temple visits. Rent sarongs locally for ₹150–400, or buy lightweight cotton ones at markets.
WEATHER

Hot & Humid Year-Round

Expect 26–32°C all year. Wet season runs November–March with afternoon downpours. Pack light fabrics and sunscreen.
MUST VISIT

Ubud, Seminyak & Beyond

Ubud rice terraces, Seminyak beaches, Nusa Penida cliffs. Don't miss sunrise at Batur volcano or Gili Islands.
