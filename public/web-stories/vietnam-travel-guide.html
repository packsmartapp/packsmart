Vietnam Travel Guide 2026

7 essentials every Indian traveler needs
VISA

E-Visa is Super Easy

Apply on evisa.gov.vn for $25–50. Single entry approved within 24 hours online.
BEST TIME

March–April & September–November

Perfect weather in these windows. North varies 8–32°C, South is warm year-round. Monsoons run May–August and December–February.
BUDGET

₹30,000–50,000 for 10 Days

Street food costs ₹50–150. Vietnam is one of Southeast Asia's cheapest destinations — pho runs just ₹60–100.
PACKING TIP

Pack Layers & Rain Gear

North can be 8–10°C in winter. South stays hot. Rain jacket is essential May–August. Quick-dry clothes save money.
CURRENCY

Watch the Zeros!

Vietnamese Dong (VND): 1 USD ≈ 25,500 VND. Use ATMs in cities. Don't exchange at airport — rates are poor. Mobile payments via Momo work everywhere.
MUST VISIT

Classic Route

Hanoi → Ha Long Bay → Hoi An → Ho Chi Minh. Take scenic trains, street food tours, and stay in charming riverside towns.
