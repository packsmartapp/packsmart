Malaysia Travel Guide 2026

7 tips every Indian traveler needs to know
VISA

eNTRI is Completely FREE

Zero cost, instant approval on entri.gov.my. Valid for 15 days, single entry. Best visa deal in Southeast Asia.
BEST TIME

December to April

Driest months across most of Malaysia. East coast monsoon runs November–February. Avoid May–October wet season.
BUDGET

₹30,000–50,000 for 7 Days

Meals cost ₹100–250. Kuala Lumpur and Penang offer amazing value — clean hostels, great transport, cheap eats everywhere.
STREET FOOD

Penang's UNESCO Hawker Stalls

Georgetown is a food lover's heaven. Kopitiam meals run MYR 5–10 (₹95–190). Char koay teow, laksa, and roti canai are legendary.
WEATHER

Hot, Humid & Rainy

High humidity year-round. Afternoon showers are common. Pack breathable fabrics, an umbrella, and waterproof bags for your phone.
MUST VISIT

KL, Penang & Langkawi

Petronas Towers in KL, Georgetown street art in Penang, and island beaches in Langkawi. Easy trains connect everything.
